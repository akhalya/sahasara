About Us

Welcome to Sahasara Wellness 

Sahasara is a paradise for those seeking to nourish their mind, body, soul, and spirit. The name "Sahasara" translates to "thousand-petal lotus," a symbol of the seventh chakra located at the crown of one’s head. This chakra controls your connection to spirit and your sense of universal consciousness, wisdom, and self-knowledge. At Sahasara Wellness, we aspire to help you radiate upwards, embracing your highest potential.

Our Mission 

At Sahasara Wellness, our mission is to help you reconnect with your roots and rediscover the healing power of nature. We believe in the importance of integrating traditional wisdom with modern wellness practices to create a holistic approach to health. By understanding and honoring the principles of alternative medicine, we provide preventive care that addresses the root causes of imbalance and dis-ease.

Our Philosophy 

We are dedicated to fostering a deep understanding of the intrinsic connection between nature and human well-being. Our philosophy is grounded in the belief that nature is a divine gift, offering boundless wisdom and healing. By reconnecting with nature, we can achieve a state of harmony and balance that transcends conventional understanding. 

Our Approach 

At Sahasara Wellness, we offer a comprehensive range of services designed to promote holistic health and well-being. Our experienced practitioners combine traditional healing practices with modern techniques to provide personalized care tailored to your unique needs. Our services include:

  • Yoga: Experience the transformative power of ancient yoga practices, including Hatha, Vinyasa, and Ashtanga, to enhance your physical and mental well-being.
  • Yoga Nidra: Indulge in deep relaxation and rejuvenation with guided meditation practices that promote complete awareness and tranquility.
  • Tibetan Sound Healing: Rebalance your chakras and harmonize your energy with the therapeutic frequencies of Tibetan sound bowls.
  • Naturopathy: Embrace natural remedies, nutrition, and lifestyle modifications to support holistic health and prevent illness.
  • Neuropathy: Benefit from specialized treatments designed to alleviate nerve pain and improve nerve function.
  • Foot Reflexology: Stimulate self-healing through gentle pressure applied to specific points on your feet, corresponding to various organs and glands.
  • Mantra Meditation: Focus your mind and elevate your spirit with the repetition of sacred Vedic sounds, leading to inner peace and clarity. 

Our Commitment 

We are committed to providing a sanctuary where you can explore and embrace the full spectrum of holistic wellness. Our goal is to empower you with the knowledge and tools to achieve optimal health and well-being. By fostering a deep connection with nature and your inner self, we help you unlock your true potential and live a life of balance and harmony.
